About Raymine Hospital

Raymine Hospital is a growing child-focused, family-friendly hospital in Port Harcourt, Rivers State. Founded in 2022, we provide both primary and secondary healthcare, with a strong emphasis on pediatrics and family medicine.

Our mission is simple: to help children reach their full potential and to support the families who care for them. We combine compassionate bedside care with modern diagnostics and are steadily integrating smart, AI-assisted tools to support clinical decision-making.
